Crispy Fried Chicken

    Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Maggi Magic sarap
  • Vegetable out
  • Freshley Ground pepper

Steps to make a Fried Chicken

  1. Season chicken with MAGGI® Magic Sarap® and pepper.
  2. Coat chicken well with flour.
  3. Pan fry in preheated oil until golden brown. Set aside to drain excess oil. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
